MOTOCROSS

The topic I want to speak about is motocross.

Motocross is an extreme sport that consist of different riders racing to be the first one to cross the final line. It is an extreme sport due to its high risk of getting injured as you are riding a motorbike at a high speed againts other riders. The most prestigious competition is the MXGP in which riders from all over the world are competing to be the World Champion.

Since I was really young, my father has always had a motorbike and he always showed me videos of him riding in the circuit near where we live.

For this reason, my dream has always been to become a professional motocross rider, but unfortunately, due to high school and external things that has to remain as a dream although I sometimes ride my personal motorbike in my free time.

The current World Champion is Jorge Prado, a Spanish guy who has won the title twice becoming the youngest rider to achieve it in history with only 23 years while competing with other legends such as Toni Cairoli or Jeffrey Herlings.
